About WordCounter

What is WordCounter?

WordCounter is a free online tool that counts words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and calculates reading time. It's a valuable tool for writers, students, journalists, and professionals who need to analyze their text content.

Why use WordCounter?

Knowing your word count helps you meet requirements for essays, articles, and social media posts. Character count is essential for platforms with character limits. Sentence and paragraph analysis helps improve readability and writing structure.

How it works

Simply type or paste your text into the input box. WordCounter instantly analyzes your text as you type, providing real-time statistics. The tool uses advanced algorithms to accurately count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs.

Word Targets

Set word count goals for your writing projects. The progress bar helps you track your progress toward common targets like 500 words (typical blog post) or 1000 words (article length). Aim for the sweet spot for your content type.
